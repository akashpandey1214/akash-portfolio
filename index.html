Research & Innovations

My research explores the fusion of NFC (Near Field Communication) tags and Artificial Intelligence in real-time health monitoring systems. It proposes a predictive health model using wearable sensors that transmit vitals like heart rate, temperature, and location to an AI engine that detects anomalies and predicts risks. This model was presented at the Kansas Data Science Conference 2025 and received appreciation from multiple academic advisors and healthcare professionals.

The innovation lies in integrating multi-source data streams with real-time analysis and predictive triggers that could alert caregivers and reduce medical response time in critical situations. This research opens new doors in patient-centered healthcare analytics and proactive intervention.
