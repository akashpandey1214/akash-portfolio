Research & Innovations

My research investigates the integration of NFC tags with AI models to build real-time healthcare monitoring solutions. Using biometric data streams like heart rate, body temperature, and oxygen levels, the system predicts health anomalies and suggests interventions before emergencies occur.

Key aspects of my research include data security through encrypted transmission, cloud-native AI model hosting, and predictive anomaly detection algorithms. This work was presented at the Kansas Data Science Conference 2025 and received commendation for addressing patient safety, operational efficiency, and proactive healthcare transformation.

I continue to refine this research with a focus on scalability, ethical AI considerations, and real-world application in telemedicine and elder care ecosystems.
