About Me

Hello, I’m Akash Pandey — a passionate Data Analyst, Web Developer, and AI Researcher based in Wichita, Kansas. With a strong background in Business Analytics and Technology, my mission is to bridge the gap between raw data and strategic decision-making. Throughout my academic journey at Wichita State University and practical internships, I have developed an integrated understanding of how technology can fuel business transformation.

What drives me is curiosity: the urge to find patterns in chaos, to solve problems creatively, and to use data ethically to make an impact. I specialize in designing data-driven systems that improve business efficiency, enhance customer experiences, and power digital transformation. My work is guided by a commitment to continuous learning, empathy for users, and excellence in execution.

Outside of analytics, I am passionate about public speaking, innovation in education technology, and contributing to initiatives that create inclusive, tech-savvy communities. My vision is to lead projects where data, technology, and human understanding converge to solve real-world problems.

Research & Innovations

My research focuses on the intersection of wearable technology, IoT, and Artificial Intelligence in healthcare systems. Specifically, I am investigating the integration of Near Field Communication (NFC) Tags and predictive AI models to monitor patient vitals and environmental conditions in real-time. The solution emphasizes proactive intervention by analyzing streaming biometric data and sending predictive alerts based on historical trends.

Presented at the Kansas Data Science Conference 2025, this project received recognition for proposing a scalable architecture that combines cloud-based storage, secure data transmission, and machine learning-based anomaly detection. My ambition is to continue expanding this research into practical deployments that empower healthcare professionals and save lives by shifting care from reactive to predictive models.
